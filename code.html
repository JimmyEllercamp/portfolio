About Me

My name is James Ellercamp. I'm a game and web developer, and a composer, with a background in professional music. I began as a self-taught coder aiming to create memorable experiences for all users with a focus on styles, animations and transitions, and completed my studies with Juno College of Technology in 21/22.

I grew up in Sydney, Australia and travelled the world before settling in Toronto, Canada. My goal is to combine my creative background as a composer, my web and game development skills to build unique, beautiful and cutting edge projects that people have difficulty forgetting.
