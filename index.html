About Me

I'm a Front-End Developer based in Toronto and I love creating vibrant interactive designs, music, and travelling.

My name is James Ellercamp. I have been coding as a Front-End Developer since 2020, and have completed Juno College of Technology's Immersive Web Development Bootcamp, and Juno's Web Dev course in '21 - '22.

My life before coding was as a composer / musician in Australia and Canada, where I wrote music for screen media, produced and performed.

Now I aim to combine the two skills to build some exciting hybrid projects in the future.
